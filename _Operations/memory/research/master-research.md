## Key Definitions (Gold Standard)

### AT Device
**IDEA/Tech Act (1988), adopted into U.S. SPED law:**
"Any item, piece of equipment, or product system, whether acquired commercially off the shelf, modified, or customized, that is used to increase, maintain, or improve functional capabilities of individuals with disabilities." (Excludes surgically implanted devices per IDEA.)

**WHO (2018):** "Products that maintain or improve an individual's functioning and independence, thereby promoting their well-being."

**Sources:** Scherer (2002), Tech Act (1988/Assistive Technology Device, 2004), WHO (2018), Peckham et al. (2018).

### Capacity-Building (RESNA)
Contrasts with "expert model." Focuses on building knowledge/skills of ALL IEP team members through coaching, group presentations, and on-demand resources. Equips everyone to take responsibility within their scope of practice.
