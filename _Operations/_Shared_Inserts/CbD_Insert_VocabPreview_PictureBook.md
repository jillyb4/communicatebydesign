### Vocabulary Preview Routine (5 minutes before reading)

Run this routine before you read. You'll use the symbol vocabulary cards from the student packet.

For each word:
1. Hold up the symbol card and say the word aloud
2. Read the child-friendly definition (printed on the card)
3. Use the word in a sentence from the book
4. Post the card where students can see it during reading

Students may respond to each word by pointing, selecting, gesturing, or saying the word — any response counts.
