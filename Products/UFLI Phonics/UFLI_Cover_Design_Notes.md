# UFLI Cover Image Design Notes (Canva)

## General Guidelines
- Use CbD brand: Navy #1B1F3B background, Electric Teal #00B4D8 for "COMMUNICATE", Amber #FFB703 for "BY DESIGN"
- Font: Arial (brand standard)
- Include CbD icon (amber speech bubble with AAC board) — pull from existing Canva brand assets
- TPT cover images: 1050 × 1350 px (standard TPT product cover)

---

## Cover 1: Teacher Guide + Communication Partner Guide

### Layout Concept
- **Top:** CbD brand bar (COMMUNICATE BY DESIGN + icon)
- **Center block:**
  - "UFLI Foundations" in white, large
  - "Teacher Guide + Communication Partner Guide" in teal, medium
  - "AAC Access Layer" in amber, accent text
- **Visual element:** Row of simple access method icons (eye, hand/touch, switch symbol, speech bubble with grid) — keep minimal, teal/amber on navy
- **Bottom strip:** "All 8 Lesson Steps | All AAC Modalities | Evidence-Based" in small white text
- **Badge/callout (optional):** "The missing piece for UFLI + AAC" — amber badge, angled

### Tone
Clean, professional, not cute. This is a clinical-grade instructional tool, not a classroom decoration. It should look like something an SLP or SPED teacher picks up and trusts immediately.

## Cover 2: Individual Lesson Packet (template — swap lesson number)

### Layout Concept
- **Top:** CbD brand bar (smaller than Teacher Guide cover)
- **Center:**
  - "UFLI Lesson [XX]" in white, large
  - Phoneme "/[sound]/" and grapheme "[letter]" in amber, prominent
  - "AAC Access Packet" in teal
- **Visual element:** One large ARASAAC symbol card mockup (tilted slightly, with border) showing a word from that lesson — makes it immediately clear what's inside
- **Bottom:** "Symbol Cards | Review Words | Heart Words" in small white text
- **Corner badge:** "$1" — amber, clear price signal

### Tone
Quick, scannable, lesson-identifiable. A teacher scrolling through TPT should be able to spot the lesson number instantly. The ARASAAC symbol card visual signals "this has real AAC materials, not just worksheets."

### Batch Production Note
Create ONE template in Canva, then duplicate 34 times. Change: lesson number, phoneme/sound, grapheme letter, and the featured symbol card image. Everything else stays identical — builds brand recognition across the line.
