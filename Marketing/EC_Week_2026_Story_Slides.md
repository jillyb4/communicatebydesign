### How to read this doc

- **Interactive element** — poll, question box, link sticker, or swipe

## Canva Build Notes

- **Background:** Always #1B1F3B (Deep Ink Navy)
- **Primary text:** White, Arial Bold
- **Accent text / headers:** #FFB703 (Amber)
- **Secondary text:** #94A3B8 (Slate)
- **Never use #00B4D8 on white** — only on navy
- **Amber bar / divider:** 4–6px thick, full width or partial
- **Brand mark:** Amber speech bubble icon + "Communicate by Design" in white, small, bottom center or bottom right on every slide
- **Size:** 1080×1920 (Stories format)
- **Polls + question boxes:** Add in the Instagram app after publishing to the Story — these are native IG stickers, not Canva elements
- **Link stickers:** Also added in the IG app, not in Canva
