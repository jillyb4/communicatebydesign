### 5. Effectiveness of Adapted Vowel-Based Literacy Instruction in Minimally Verbal Adolescents with Autism and Intellectual and Developmental Disability

- **Journal:** Journal of Communication Disorders (2026)
- **DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jcomdis.2026.106640
- **PMID:** 41950804 | Published: March 27, 2026
- **Topics:** AAC, Literacy, Phonics / Structured Literacy, Complex Communicators
- **Access:** Paywalled — need
