## 🆓 AT Consideration Checklist — Free

*The one you can use at your next IEP meeting.*

IDEA requires AT consideration at every IEP meeting and every reevaluation [34 CFR §300.324(a)(2)(v)]. This two-page checklist operationalizes that requirement: 12 functional areas, structured team documentation, and a decision record (AT needed / trial recommended / not needed with rationale).

This is your compliance baseline. It's free because every team needs it regardless of budget.

## A Note on How These Tools Are Designed

None of these are "specialist tasks wrapped in a form." They're capacity-building tools — meaning they're designed to give every member of the IEP team the knowledge and structure they need to take responsibility for what's in their scope of practice.

The AT specialist can't be everywhere. And in most schools, there isn't one. These tools are built for that reality.

Everything is editable. Everything is grounded in IDEA and evidence-based practice. Everything is designed to be used, not filed.
